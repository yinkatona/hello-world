German Shepherd Dog (GSD)

Confidence, Courageous and Smart

  • Origin: Germany
  • Weight: 22-33kg(Female); 30-40kg(Male)
  • Litter Size: 4-9
  • Lifespan: 9-13years

Also known as Alsatian in Britain and other European countries, is one of the top 10 most populous breed in the US and probably the most recognised breed in the world. There are many reasons why GSD stand in the front rank of canine royalty, but their defining attribute is character: loyalty, courage, confidence, the ability to learn command for new task and the willingness to put self in line of defence for loved once. GSD will make a gentle family dog and steadfast guardian
